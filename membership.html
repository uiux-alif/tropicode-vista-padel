FAQ

Common questions

Is there a joining fee?+
No joining fee, ever. You simply pay the monthly plan rate and your membership is active. Cancel anytime with no penalty.
How do I cancel my membership?+
Simply message us on WhatsApp at least 7 days before your next billing cycle and we'll cancel it — no questions asked, no fees, no hassle.
How does priority booking work?+
Vista Members get 48-hour early access to booking slots before they open to the public. Training Pack members get 72-hour early access. This means peak hours (17:00–22:00) are almost always available to members first.
Can I share my membership with someone?+
Memberships are individual and non-transferable. However, you can always bring guests to play with you — guest court fees apply at standard rates.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We accept bank transfer (BCA, Mandiri, BNI), GoPay, OVO, and DANA. Payment details are confirmed via WhatsApp after your membership application is reviewed.
Do coaching sessions in the Training Pack expire?+
The 4 monthly coaching sessions must be used within the calendar month. Unused sessions do not roll over to the next month, so we recommend scheduling early!
Are member events free?+
Most member events — including the monthly social match night — are free for all Vista Members and Training Pack holders. Special tournament events may have a small entry fee.
